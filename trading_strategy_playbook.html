CORE SYSTEM

The HTF-First
Framework

Every trade decision flows from a strict top-down analysis. High timeframes define the narrative. Lower timeframes provide the precision. Never the other way around.

STEP 01
🗺️
HTF Range Definition
Define range high and low on Weekly/Daily. Pull Fibonacci from swing high to swing low. Above 0.5 = Premium zone. Below 0.5 = Discount zone.
STEP 02
🏗️
Structure Narrative
Identify Break of Structure (BOS) on HTF to confirm directional intent. No HTF story means no trade. Full stop.
STEP 03
🎯
POI Identification
Mark Order Blocks, FVGs, Breaker Blocks and Supply/Demand zones on 4H to 20H timeframes. These are the reaction levels.
STEP 04
💧
Liquidity Hunt
Confirm liquidity sweep or last liquidity of range. Smart money manipulation must be present. This is the fuel for the real move.
STEP 05
⚡
LTF Entry Trigger
Drop to LTF for confirmation: displacement, micro BOS, or entry model trigger. Sniper entry with defined invalidation below/above structure.
A+ SETUP CRITERIA

10-Point Confluence
Checklist

A valid entry requires the convergence of multiple independent factors. The more factors aligned, the higher the probability. Trade only A+ setups — everything else is noise.

01
Break of Structure
MTF or HTF BOS confirms directional intent and continuation of buyer or seller control.
02
Breaker Blocks
Aligned across main TFs (4H, 6H, 8H, 12H+) confirming institutional algorithmic reaction levels.
03
Fibonacci Levels
0.618, 0.705, 0.786, and 0.5 equilibrium levels confirm algorithmic discount or premium of previous swing.
04
Premium / Discount
Only trade from optimal side of the range curve. Never from mid-range. Position matters above all.
05
Volume Profile / OFA
Confirms fair pricing and identifies high or low volume reaction nodes for precision targeting.
06
Order Blocks / S&D
HTF Supply and Demand zones likely to cause reversal or continuation. Used as structural support/resistance.
07
Liquidity Sweep
Smart money manipulation confirmed. Last liquidity of range taken, providing the fuel for the directional move.
08
LTF / MTF Entry
Precise entry confirmation on lower timeframes. Sniper timing with clear, logical invalidation point.
09
Relative Strength
DXY and majors confluence confirms macro alignment. Avoids fighting stronger pairs or divergent indices.
10
SL & TP Mapping
Logical stop below or above liquidity with pre-mapped targets. Asymmetric risk-to-reward is non-negotiable.

Account size does not change how strict risk management must be. The habits formed on a small account are the same habits that protect seven-figure capital. Trade today as if you were already handling six figures.
"If you can't protect $1,000, you can't protect $1,000,000." — Master
✂️
Cut Early, Recover Fast
Losses expand geometrically. A 50% drawdown requires a 100% gain just to break even. If a level breaks, cut the position. Do not justify, rationalize, or "believe in" a losing trade. Hope is not a strategy.
"Hope isn't a strategy. It's a slow death." — Master

"Better to leave profit on the table than hope for it to come back." — Master

NO-TRADE RULES

10 Reasons to
Stay Out

Knowing when not to trade is as valuable as knowing when to enter. These rules are hard stops. No exceptions, no negotiations.

RULE 01
No HTF Narrative
If liquidity, BOS, or directional bias is not clear on HTF, the setup is invalid. No HTF story equals no entry.
RULE 02
Wrong Location
If price is not at Premium/Discount, SD zone, POI, or clear range extreme — skip immediately.
RULE 03
Red-Folder News
FOMC, CPI, NFP, Fed speeches, Rate Decisions within ±30 minutes means no entry and close all open positions.
RULE 04
Session Open Volatility
No new trades in the first 5 to 15 minutes of NY or London open. Wait for direction after the volatility sweep.
RULE 05
Bad Mental State
If tired, tilted, greedy, fearful, impatient, or revenge-minded — trading is forbidden. Full stop.
RULE 06
No Valid Invalidation
If a logical structure-based stop loss cannot be clearly defined, the setup is not valid. No SL, no trade.
RULE 07
Overtrading Impulse
Daily limit reached or urge to increase size means stop immediately. More trades means more exposure, not more edge.
RULE 08
Choppy / No Displacement
No momentum, low volume, or unclear trend means avoid chop entirely and wait for clear displacement.
RULE 09
Confluence Conflict
If DXY, majors, or relative strength contradict the trade direction, conditions are misaligned.
RULE 10
Not My Model
If the setup does not fit the system time window, entry model, or A+ criteria — it is not this system's trade.
